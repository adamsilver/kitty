Form Validator

Fully accessible and flexible form validation pattern and script.

Notes

  • An error summary appears at the top with links to the fields
  • Validation occurs on submit and moves focus to the summary
  • Errors are injected into labels for assistive devices and visual users alike
  • Fields that use a fieldset (radios or checkboxes) inject errors into the legend, for assistive devices and visual users alike
